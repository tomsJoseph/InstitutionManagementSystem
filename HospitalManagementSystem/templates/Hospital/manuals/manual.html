Personal and department profiles

Personal profile
Login > go to person's profile

For all users, personal profile allows changing personal details, sending message, filing complaints and apply leave of absence.
For doctors, personal profile allows :

  • patient consulting
  • surgery reports
  • death report
  • autopsies in detail
  • Surgeries in detail
For department managers, personal profile allows managerial tasks namely,
  • Review attendance
  • Manage leave application
  • Change staff department and shift
  • Make announcement
  • Register new staff
  • Staff reports
  • Manage complaints
For executives, personal profile allows executive tasks such as,
  • View statistics
  • View department reports
  • View staff reports and statistics
  • Manage salary
  • Manage complaints
  • Manage disciplinary actions

Login

login
Login requires a username and password. On Successful login, the user will be redirected to another page. If the credentials are wrong login will be terminated.
Select profile
This is the page that a successful login will redirect to. A non executive staff can choose between personal and department profile here. An executive staff will not have this page.
Reset password
For this to work there should be:
  • Internet connection
  • A valid email attached to the user account

There is link in login page to reset password which will redirect to a page requiring user to enter his email that is attached to his account. Once it's done successfully the user will be redirected to a page to enter the otp which would be sent to his email. If the otp sent by software in email matches with the one user enters, user will be asked to create a new password. He can then login.

Personal messaging

User can sent messages to other users confidentialy. This message will be available only for the sender and the recipients and no one else can access these.
Inbox
Login > personal profile > messaging

Here all messages sent to the user will be shown. Unread messages will be marked new and read ones will be marked read. Clicking on a message will show it in details.

new message
Login > personal profile > messaging > click on the new message button

Select recipient, preferably mention a subject not necessarily though. Enter message content and click send button.

sent box
Login > personal profile > messaging > click on the sentbox button

Here all messages sent by the user will be shown. messages unread by receiver will be marked new and read ones will be marked read. Clicking on a message will show it in details

Complaints and feedbacks

Satffs and patients can file complaints or give feedbacks.
User Complaints and feedbacks
Login > department profile > complaints and feedbacks

To file a complaint or feedback, the user/patient must be registered. On the complaint or feedback form, the concerned department and concerned staff are to be selected. If the complaint is personally against a staff and not any department, the department of the concerned staff has to be selected as the concerned department. If the patient is filing a complaint, the is_complaint checkbox has to be ticked.

Staff Complaints and feedbacks
Login > personal profile > complaints and feedbacks

This will lead to a page that showing the staff's old complaints or feedback, if any. To file a complaint or feedback, the staff should select new complaint/feedback option. On the complaint or feedback form, the concerned department and concerned staff are to be selected. If the complaint is personally against a staff and not any department, the department of the concerned staff has to be selected as the concerned department. If the staff is filing a complaint, the is_complaint checkbox has to be ticked.

Editing Complaints and feedbacks
Only staff complaints can be edited. Editing user complaints is not allowed to ensure that the staffs don't tamper them without the knowledge of patient. User complaint once filed can only be viewed by the concerned department manager.
To Edit staff complaint:
Login > staff profile > complaints and feedbacks

This will lead to a page that showing the staff's old complaints or feedback, if any. To edit a complaint or feedback, the staff should click on a complaint/feedback this will lead to an edit page.

Managing Complaints and feedbacks
Complaints or feedback can only be managed by the concerned department manager and Executive staff.
Management of complaints or feedback for department manager:
Login > staff profile > complaints and feedbacks

This will lead to a page that showing the complaints and feedbacks filed my the manager along with the complaints and feedbacks concerning his/her department. Clicking on a complaint or feedback concerning his department will lead to a detailed page. On this page the manager can add his comments and mark the complaint as concluded or not concluded.

Management of complaints or feedbacks for executive staff
Login > departments > select 'see department report' of any department > in the report page click see complaints at the bottom of the report.

This will lead to a page where all complaints against the particular department. clicking on a complaint will lead to a manage complaints form. Clicking on a complaint or feedback concerning his department will lead to a detailed page. On this page the executive staff can mark the complaint as concluded or not concluded.

Notifications

Notifications appear on both notification bar and home page. Notifications regarding new messages appear on notification bar. Important notifications such as surgeries and announcements appear on home screen of concerned staff and concerned deparmtent.
message notifications

clicking on a new message will show detailed message. once read, new personal message will disappear from notification bar of staff home page. However, department message will stay on the notification bar of department page for a day even if it's read.

surgery notifications

Once completed or canceled, surgery notification will disappear from home page.

Announcement notifications

Announcement notification will disappear from home page after a day.

Surgery

Only doctors can access these function. once surgery is initiated, untill it's finished there will be a alert message in the concerned department and personal home pages.
Initiate a surgery
To do this the patient must be in doctor's department
Login > staff profile > patient consulting> search using patient number > select appropriate record > go to patient report > mark the patient for surgery at the bottom of page
edit surgery information
To do this the patient must be in doctor's department or the doctor must be in the surgery team or the doctor must the surgery inititator.
there are two ways:
  • For surgery initiator
    Login > staff profile > patient consulting> search using patient number > select appropriate record > go to patient report > in the surgeries section click on the edit link of concerned surgery
  • For team members
    Login > staff profile > surgeries> select the surgery > edit surgery information

This will lead to the surgery form. Here new information can be added and existing ones edited. There is an option to delete surgery however on completion of surgery, deletion will be impossible.

Write/edit surgery report
To do this the doctor must be in the surgery team or must be the one that initiated the surgery.
  • for team members
    Login > staff profile > surgeries> select the surgery > edit report
  • only for the doctor who initiated surgery
    Login > staff profile > patient consulting> search using patient number > select appropriate record > go to patient report > in the surgeries section click on the write report link of concerned surgery
add/remove surgery docs
To do this the doctor must be in the surgery team or must be the one that initiated the surgery.
go to surgery report > click on edit files link in the documents section.
